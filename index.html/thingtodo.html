visit Shree Antu

Shree Antu around 2,300 m above sea level), one of the untouched destinations for tourists, is a town in Ilam District in Mechi Zone of Province 1, Nepal, right at the border to Darjeeling/India and south of Kanchenjunga (8586 m), the world’s third highest mountain. It is one of the tourist destination best known as the place to observe sunrise and is also famous for tea gardens. The beautiful sunrise view can be observed from view tower, which is been constructed at top of the Shree Antu Hill situated at an altitude of 2328 m. Homestay, alongside of the pound, also aids to make is one of the prefect place to visit for both national and international tourists. Originally, Nepali tea takes off was being sold to production lines in Darjeeling, to meet their rising demands, being a profitable input for the production lines in and around Darjeeling. Few years afterward another preparing plant was open in Soktim, Jhapa.
